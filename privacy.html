Privacy Policy

Privpost.online values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Note content and attachments (deleted after expiry or being read)
  • All uploaded images are automatically stripped of metadata (EXIF, ICC, etc.) for privacy
  • IP address (for anti-spam and abuse prevention, not shared)
  • Basic usage statistics (number of notes, views, etc.)

How we use your data

  • To provide the self-destructing note service
  • To prevent spam and abuse
  • To improve the service (aggregate statistics only)

Data retention

  • Notes and files are deleted after being read or expired
  • IP addresses are stored for 12h for anti-spam, then deleted
  • Statistics are stored in aggregate form only

Cookies

Privpost.online does not use tracking cookies. Session cookies are used only for admin login.

Contact
