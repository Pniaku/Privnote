About Privpost.online

Privpost.online is a free, privacy-focused tool for sending confidential, self-destructing notes and files. Our mission is to make secure, one-time sharing of sensitive information simple and accessible for everyone.

How it works

  • Create a note or upload a file (max 50MB, 50,000 characters).
  • Optionally set a password, expiry time, or limit the number of views.
  • Share the generated link with your recipient.
  • Once the note is read (or the view/expiry limit is reached), it is permanently destroyed from our servers.

Privacy & Security

  • No registration required. We do not track users or store unnecessary data.
  • Notes and files are deleted after reading or expiry.
  • Optional password protection and burn-after-views for extra security.
  • All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS.
